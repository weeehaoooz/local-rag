# Company Profile: Vanguard BioTech

**Name**: Vanguard BioTech
**Industry**: Biotechnology / Life Sciences
**Headquarters**: Boston, USA (Main Campus)
**Operations**: R&D labs located in Biopolis, Singapore.
**Founded**: 2015
**Website**: www.vanguardbiotech.org
**Mission**: "Revolutionizing genomic medicine to heal the world's rarest diseases."

## Overview
Vanguard BioTech is a clinical-stage biotechnology company pioneering next-generation genomic medicines. We leverage mRNA technology and CRISPR-Cas9 gene editing to develop therapies for rare pediatric genetic disorders that have traditionally had no known cure.

## Focus Areas
* **Rare Disease Therapeutics**: Targeted therapies for Spinal Muscular Atrophy (SMA) and Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy (DMD).
* **mRNA Vaccines**: Developing a universal flu vaccine platform and therapeutic vaccines for certain types of oncology.
* **Precision Diagnostics**: AI-powered genomic sequencing to identify disease markers earlier than conventional liquid biopsies.
* **Gene Editing R&D**: Advancing next-gen CRISPR tools for more precise base editing with minimal off-target effects.

## Key People
* **Dr. Elena Rodriguez (Chief Scientific Officer)**: Renowned geneticist and Nobel laureate in Medicine for her foundational work on CRISPR-Cas9.
* **James Montgomery (CEO)**: Serial biotech entrepreneur with a track record of driving three startups from seed stage to IPO.
* **Prof. Kevin Zhou (Chief Data Scientist)**: Specializes in deep learning for drug discovery and structural biology.

## Key Collaborations
* **Academic**: MIT, Harvard Medical School, National University of Singapore (NUS), and Duke-NUS Medical School.
* **Industrial**: Merck, Thermo Fisher Scientific, and A*STAR.

## Recent News & Milestones
* **Phase 2 Clinical Trials for V-Gen 1 (2024)**: Showed significant improvement in muscle regeneration and motor function in SMA patients during initial cohorts.
* **FDA Breakthrough Therapy Designation**: Granted for its lead oncology candidate targeting HER2-positive breast cancer.
* **Singapore Biopolis Expansion**: Doubled R&D lab space to focus on pandemic preparedness and emerging viral threats.

## Values
Scientific Excellence, Patient First, and Open Collaboration. Our "Open-Gene" initiative allows academic researchers to access our proprietary sequencing tools for non-commercial use.
