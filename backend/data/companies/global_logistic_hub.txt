# Company Profile: Global Logistic Hub

**Name**: Global Logistic Hub
**Industry**: Logistics & Supply Chain Management
**Headquarters**: Singapore
**Founded**: 1995
**Website**: www.globallogistichub.com
**Mission**: "Leading the world in seamless, sustainable, and data-driven logistical solutions."

## Overview
Global Logistic Hub is a global leader in third-party logistics (3PL) and supply chain management services. Based in Singapore, we operate a vast network of warehouses, distribution centers, and transportation fleets across 15 countries. We specialize in cross-border e-commerce fulfillment, cold chain logistics, and project cargo.

## Core Services
* **E-Commerce Fulfillment**: End-to-end logistics solutions for online retailers, from warehousing to last-mile delivery and reverse logistics.
* **Cold Chain Logistics**: Specialized handling and transportation of temperature-sensitive goods for the pharmaceutical and food industries.
* **Supply Chain Optimization**: Using advanced data analytics and AI to optimize inventory management, transportation routing, and demand forecasting.
* **Sustainable Warehousing**: Implementing green-certified warehouse designs and zero-emission transportation fleets.

## Key Strategic Partners
* **Maersk**: Joint venture for integrated maritime and inland logistics operations.
* **Alibaba Group (Cainiao)**: Strategic partner for cross-border e-commerce hubs in Southeast Asia.
* **FedEx**: Collaboration on international air freight and express delivery services.
* **PSA Singapore**: Integrated port-to-warehouse logistics and automation projects.
