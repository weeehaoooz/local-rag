# Company Profile: Apex Global Solutions

**Name**: Apex Global Solutions
**Industry**: Enterprise Software & IT Consulting
**Headquarters**: Singapore
**Founded**: 2012
**Website**: www.apexglobalsolutions.com
**Mission**: "Empowering businesses through cutting-edge AI and cloud-native solutions."

## Overview
Apex Global Solutions is a premier IT consulting firm based in Singapore, serving clients across Southeast Asia and Europe. We specialize in digital transformation, helping legacy businesses migrate to modern cloud infrastructures while integrating advanced data analytics into their core operations.

## Core Services
* **Cloud Migration**: Seamless transition of on-premise workloads to AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud with specialized focus on high-availability architectures.
* **AI & Machine Learning**: Custom Large Language Model (LLM) fine-tuning, predictive maintenance models, and automated customer support systems.
* **Cybersecurity**: End-to-end security audits, threat detection, and zero-trust architecture implementation for regulated industries.
* **Data Warehousing**: Scalable data lake solutions built on Snowflake and Databricks, enabling real-time business intelligence.

## Key Clients
* **DBS Bank**: Implemented regional real-time fraud detection systems.
* **Singtel**: Modernized 5G infrastructure management platforms.
* **Standard Chartered**: Migrated core retail banking systems to a hybrid cloud environment.
* **Grab**: Optimized data ingestion pipelines for their multi-service super-app.
