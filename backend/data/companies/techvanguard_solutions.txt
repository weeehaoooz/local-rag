# Company Profile: TechVanguard Solutions

**Name**: TechVanguard Solutions
**Industry**: Cybersecurity & Enterprise Infrastructure
**Headquarters**: Singapore
**Founded**: 2015
**Website**: www.techvanguard.solutions
**Mission**: "Securing the future of digital enterprises with zero-trust and quantum-ready solutions."

## Overview
TechVanguard Solutions is a leading cybersecurity firm headquartered in Singapore, providing advanced threat protection and infrastructure security services to government agencies, financial institutions, and multinational corporations across Asia Pacific. We specialize in zero-trust architectures, identity and access management (IAM), and advanced encryption techniques.

## Core Services
* **Zero-Trust Implementation**: Comprehensive architecture design and deployment of zero-trust security frameworks for distributed workforces.
* **Incident Response & Digital Forensics**: Rapid-response teams to mitigate cyber threats and conduct post-incident investigations.
* **Quantum-Safe Encryption**: Developing and deploying next-generation encryption standards to protect sensitive data against emerging quantum computing threats.
* **Managed Security Services (MSSP)**: 24/7 security monitoring through our state-of-the-art Security Operations Centers (SOC).

## Key Clients
