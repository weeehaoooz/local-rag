# Company Profile: EcoGrowth Partners

**Name**: EcoGrowth Partners
**Industry**: Sustainable Agriculture & Green Technology Venture Capital
**Headquarters**: Singapore
**Founded**: 2018
**Website**: www.ecogrowthpartners.vc
**Mission**: "Investing in the future of sustainable food systems and renewable energy resources."

## Overview
EcoGrowth Partners is a venture capital firm dedicated to supporting early-stage startups and growth-oriented companies in the fields of agricultural technology (AgTech), climate technology (CliTech), and circular economy solutions. We bridge the gap between traditional investment and impactful environmental regeneration.

## Core Services
* **Venture Capital Funding**: Providing seed and series-A funding for startups demonstrating disruptive potential in sustainable technologies.
* **Impact Advisory**: Assisting portfolio companies in measuring and optimizing their environmental and social governance (ESG) performance.
* **Technology Scoping**: Identifying and evaluating emerging agricultural technologies for integration into regional food security frameworks.
* **Strategic Partnerships**: Facilitating collaborations between startups and large-scale industrial players to accelerate green transitions.

## Key Portfolio Companies
* **HydroFarm Asia**: Pioneers in vertically integrated urban hydroponic systems.
* **SolarGrid Solutions**: Developing distributed solar microgrids for rural electrification.
* **AlgaeFuel Labs**: Innovating biofuels and biological carbon sequestration techniques.
* **RecycleGo**: Digital platform for waste-to-energy conversion and circular supply chain management.

## Key People
* **Li Mei Hua (Managing Partner)**: Former investment director at a global sovereign wealth fund and an advocate for impact investing.
* **Dr. Robert Green (CTO)**: Leading expert in soil science and sustainable agricultural practices with over 20 years of research experience.

## Recent Achievements
* **Global Impact Investor Award (2023)**: For outstanding contribution to sustainable food security initiatives.
* **B Corp Certification**: Recognized for high standards of social and environmental performance, transparency, and accountability.

## Company Culture & Values
"Sustainability through Synthesis." We believe in a holistic approach to investment, where financial returns are inseparable from environmental stewardship and social equity. Our "EcoGrowth Fellowship" supports young environmental scientists and entrepreneurs.
