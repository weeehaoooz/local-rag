# Company Profile: GreenHorizon Energy

**Name**: GreenHorizon Energy
**Industry**: Renewable Energy & Sustainability
**Headquarters**: Sydney, Australia
**Founded**: 2018
**Website**: www.greenhorizon.energy
**Mission**: "Accelerating the world's transition to sustainable energy."

## Overview
GreenHorizon Energy is a fast-growing renewable energy company dedicated to developing, building, and operating utility-scale solar and wind power plants. Our focus is on the Asia-Pacific (APAC) region, where energy demand is rapidly rising alongside the urgent need for carbon neutrality and grid stability.

## Core Projects
* **The Great Outback Solar Farm (Western Australia)**: One of Australia's largest solar installations, powering over 50,000 homes annually and reducing carbon emissions by 150,000 tons.
* **Singapore Rooftop Initiative (Ongoing)**: A partnership with JTC to install high-efficiency solar panels on industrial buildings across Jurong Island.
* **Jakarta Offshore Wind Project**: Currently in the feasibility and permitting stage, aiming to provide 500MW of clean energy to Indonesia's power grid by 2027.
* **Hydro-Pumped Storage (Tasmania)**: Developing long-duration energy storage solutions to support grid reliability.

## Technology Focus
* **BESS (Battery Energy Storage Systems)**: Advanced lithium-ion and vanadium flow battery technologies designed to stabilize intermittent renewable energy output.
* **Solar Cell Innovation**: R&D into perovskite solar cells for higher efficiency in tropical climates.
* **Smart Grid Orchestration**: Proprietary AI-driven software to manage variable energy loads across diverse regional grids in real-time.

## Key People
* **David O'Sullivan (Managing Director)**: A veteran of the energy sector with 25 years of experience in both fossil fuels and renewables. He previously led BP's APAC renewable ventures.
* **Nurul Aishah (Head of Regional Operations)**: Leads all Southeast Asian project execution, based in the Singapore regional headquarters. She is an expert in regional regulatory compliance.

## Recent News
* **Strategic Partnership with Temasek (2024)**: Secured $500M in Series C funding to expand into India and Vietnam.
* **United Nations Global Compact**: Joined as a lead participant in the Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) for Clean Energy.

## Values
Sustainability, Efficiency, and Community Empowerment. We prioritize hiring local workers for project construction and maintenance, particularly in remote areas.
